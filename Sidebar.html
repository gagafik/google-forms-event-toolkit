Limit slots on your form options

Set a maximum number of registrations for any multiple-choice or checkbox option. When the limit is reached, that option is automatically removed from your form.

Example:
"Morning session — 20 spots" → automatically closes when 20 people register.
⚙️

How it works

  1. You pick a question and option, set a slot limit
  2. We watch for new submissions in real time
  3. When the slot fills up, the option disappears from your form automatically
  4. You get an email alert at 75%, 90%, and 100% full
Note: In rare cases of simultaneous submissions, 1–2 extra responses may be accepted before the slot closes. This is disclosed in your setup.
